What are Built-In Functions?

These are predefined routines packed directly inside Python. They stand instantly ready for execution without requiring structural definition steps.

ANATOMY OF A CALL

len()

Counts the exact index volume or sequence item summation. (e.g., measuring string lengths).

3

Aggregates and totals continuous numeric clusters into one comprehensive variable evaluation.
